Introduction

CyberConnect Podcast is an educational initiative focused on raising awareness about cybersecurity, digital laws, artificial intelligence risks, and emerging technological threats in Ghana. Through expert discussions and research-based insights, the podcast aims to simplify complex cybersecurity topics for a broad audience.

Problem Statement

Cyber threats in Ghana continue to escalate, ranging from misinformation campaigns to the unauthorized distribution of private content. Many citizens remain unaware of the legal implications under Ghana’s Cybersecurity Act 2020, leading to increasing digital exploitation and vulnerabilities.

Recent cyber incidents affecting national infrastructure demonstrate the urgent need for digital awareness and proactive security measures. Emerging technologies, particularly artificial intelligence, further introduce new and evolving risks.

Objectives

The primary objective of CyberConnect Podcast is to educate listeners about the provisions of Ghana’s Cybersecurity Act 2020 and promote responsible digital behavior.

The podcast also analyzes major cyber incidents in Ghana, explores preventive strategies, and highlights career opportunities within the cybersecurity industry.

Significance

In a rapidly digitizing economy, cybersecurity awareness is essential. By collaborating with experts and referencing institutions such as Ghana’s Cybersecurity Authority, the podcast contributes to national efforts aimed at strengthening digital security and building a more resilient society.
